Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your visit to the original Salón Monterrey.
Remember: We have no branches.

  • Our History

    Founded in 1879, we are among the founding establishments of the cantina tradition in El Parián, a building originally designed for seed commerce.

  • How to get here?

    We are located at Progreso #21, Tlaquepaque. There are several safe parking lots nearby, or you can walk along the Independencia pedestrian walkway.

  • Do I need a reservation?

    It's not mandatory, we always find a spot for you. However, for groups larger than 10 people, we recommend letting us know so we can give you the best location.

  • Mariachi at your table

    In addition to the general shows at El Parián, you can request the Mariachi to play your favorite songs directly at your table for an additional cost.

  • Show Times

    The festive atmosphere starts in the afternoon. The folk dance shows and mariachi performances usually have fixed lunch and dinner schedules.

  • Cazuelas and Drinks

    Our signature drink is the "Cazuela," available in different sizes. We also serve ice-cold national beers and traditional Mexican cocktails.
