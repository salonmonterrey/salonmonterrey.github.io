Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your visit to the original Salón Monterrey.
Note: We do not have other branches; accept no imitations.

  • Our History

    Founded in 1905, we are one of the founding establishments of the cantina tradition in El Parián, a building dating back to 1890 originally designed as a seed market.

  • How to get here?

    We are located at Progreso #21, Tlaquepaque. There are several secure parking lots nearby, or you can arrive by walking through the Independencia pedestrian walkway.

  • Do I need a reservation?

    It is not mandatory; we always strive to find a table for you. However, for groups larger than 10 people, we recommend letting us know in advance.

  • Mariachi at your table

    In addition to the general shows at El Parián, you can request the Mariachi to play your favorite songs directly at your table for an additional cost.

  • Show Times

    The festive atmosphere starts in the afternoon. Folklore dance shows and main Mariachi performances usually have fixed schedules during lunch and dinner.

  • Cazuelas & Drinks

    Our signature drink is the "Cazuela Tlaquepaque." We also serve ice-cold national beers and traditional Mexican cocktails.
